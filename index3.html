By clicking on the map, you will be able to visualize how rivers connected to the Amazonas river and its surrounding areas have changed since the 70s. At this stage, Machine Learning becomes a tool that allows us to “fill in the gaps,” recreating missing data from scattered frames. In this way, we can clearly understand how the area has transformed, alongside the river bed itself, for the last 50 years.
